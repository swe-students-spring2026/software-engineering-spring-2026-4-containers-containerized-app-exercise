🗑️ Garbage Classification Guide

Learn how to sort different types of waste correctly

♻️ Recyclable Waste (Blue Bin)

Examples:

  • Plastic bottles, containers
  • Paper, cardboard, newspapers
  • Glass bottles and jars
  • Metal cans, aluminum foil
  • Clean clothes and textiles

🔴 Hazardous Waste (Red Bin)

Examples:

  • Batteries
  • Expired medicines
  • Paint, chemicals, pesticides
  • Fluorescent lamps and bulbs
  • Electronic waste (phones, chargers)

🟢 Food / Kitchen Waste (Green Bin)

Examples:

  • Fruit peels, vegetable scraps
  • Rice, leftovers, spoiled food
  • Tea bags, coffee grounds
  • Egg shells, bones
  • Garden waste

⚫ Other / General Waste (Gray Bin)

Examples:

  • Tissue paper, napkins
  • Dirty plastic bags
  • Cigarette butts
  • Dust, hair, sweeping waste
  • Contaminated packaging
